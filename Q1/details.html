POSTER PRESENTATION DETAILS

Students who wish to participate should form a team of 3 to present the competition. The poster should have visual aids. The topics of posters selected must be technical. The abstract of the posters must be submitted on or before 3rd October, 2020. Fur further information, contact: 9876543210

The proceedings of the conference will be published in Smart Innovation Systems and Technologies Series, Springer (Approved). Extended versions of selected best papers will be expected to appear in SCI/Scopus indexed international journals.


Conference Focus Areas

  • Machine Learning
  • Internet of Things
  • Artificial Nueral Networks
  • Computational Intelligence
  • Data Science

Submission guidelines

Submission of poster must be unique and should not have been previously published or under consideration for publication. At least one of the authors of each accepted paper must register for the conference. All submissions are to be done online through easychair link.
